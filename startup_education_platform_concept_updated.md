# Startup Concept Document

## Project Vision

Create an AI-powered study platform that fundamentally changes how
students approach learning. The system generates personalized study
paths for each learner based on their curriculum, grades, exam
deadlines, and goals.

The platform acts as a **private tutor for every subject in one place**,
helping students study more efficiently and strategically.

Over time the platform can become: - A **global learning ecosystem** - A
**ranking system for students** - A **signal for universities to
evaluate applicants** - A **community hub for builders and learners**

The mission is to **rethink education and optimize how people learn for
the future.**

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Core Product Features

## 1. AI Study Path Generator

The AI analyzes: - Student curriculum - Current grades - Exam
deadlines - Learning speed - Personal goals

It then generates a **dynamic study roadmap** that updates as the
student progresses.

Example:

Math → Physics → Advanced Topics → Exam Preparation

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 2. AI Private Tutor

Each subject has an integrated AI tutor that can: - Explain concepts -
Generate practice problems - Adapt difficulty - Track progress

The tutor becomes a **personal learning companion**.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 3. Smart Exam Preparation

The system automatically prioritizes what the student should study based
on:

-   Exam date proximity
-   Knowledge gaps
-   Difficulty level
-   Historical performance

This allows students to **focus only on the highest impact material**.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 4. University Recommendation Engine

Based on student performance and rankings in the platform, the system
suggests:

-   Universities where acceptance chances are high
-   Programs matching the student's strengths
-   Application strategies

Long term vision: universities may consider platform ranking signals.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 5. Student Ranking System

Students earn scores based on:

-   Learning progress
-   Challenge completion
-   Hackathon participation
-   Knowledge assessments

A global ranking leaderboard encourages **healthy competition and
motivation**.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 6. Hackathons and Events

The platform will host:

-   Learning challenges
-   Global hackathons
-   Study competitions
-   Collaborative problem-solving events

Events help build a **community of ambitious learners and builders**.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Gamification System

To encourage continuous learning and engagement, the platform will
include **gamification mechanics** similar to successful learning apps.

Features include:

-   **XP (experience points)** for completed study sessions
-   **Level progression** as students master subjects
-   **Achievement badges** for milestones
-   **Streak tracking** for daily study habits
-   **Leaderboards** comparing students globally or within groups
-   **Challenge modes** where students compete in learning tasks

Gamification should make learning feel like **progressing through levels
in a game**, motivating students to continue improving.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Integrated Study Techniques

The platform will incorporate **scientifically proven learning methods**
to improve study efficiency.

Examples:

### Pomodoro Timer

Built-in Pomodoro sessions:

-   25 minute focused study blocks
-   5 minute breaks
-   longer breaks after several sessions

### Spaced Repetition

Review material at optimized intervals to maximize memory retention.

### Active Recall

Encourage students to test their knowledge frequently rather than
passive reading.

These tools help students **study smarter, not just longer**.

# Color System

## Primary Color

Gold Orange

Example value:

`#F59E0B`

Why: - energy - achievement - progress - motivation

Gold/orange visually represents **milestones and accomplishments**.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Light Mode

Background color:

Soft white

`#F8FAFC`

This keeps the interface:

-   clean
-   minimal
-   modern

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Dark Mode

Dark mode should be available through a **light/dark toggle**.

Background color:

Charcoal blue

`#0F172A`

In dark mode:

-   primary orange should appear slightly brighter
-   text becomes light neutral tones
-   accent colors adapt for contrast

This mode improves usability during **night studying and long
sessions**.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
